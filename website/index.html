Frequently Asked Questions

Is Quick-Saver free to use?

Yes, Quick-Saver is completely free to download and use.

How does Quick-Saver make money?

Quick-Saver uses Amazon's affiliate program. When you make a purchase through our links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

Which countries does Quick-Saver support?

Quick-Saver currently supports UK, Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Netherlands, Belgium, Sweden, and Poland Amazon marketplaces.

Is my data safe with Quick-Saver?

We value your privacy. Quick-Saver only accesses the information needed to compare prices and doesn't collect personal data.
